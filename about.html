Sher-Sharaba Kya Hai?

Sher-Sharaba Hindi sahitya ke prati samarpit ek anokha manch hai jahan kavi, kahaniakar aur sahitya premi ek saath aate hain. Hamara uddeshya Hindi bhasha aur sahitya ko digital yug mein nayi unchaiyon tak pahunchana hai.

Yahan aap na keval pratibhashali lekhakon aur unki rachnaon ko khoj sakte hain, balki apni kritiyon ko bhi share kar sakte hain. Hum niyamit roop se open mic events, kavita paath aur sahityik karyakram aayojit karte hain.

Hamari Yatra

Sher-Sharaba ki shuruaat 2024 mein Faridabad aur Noida Extension mein hui thi. Kuch sahitya premiyon ke chhote group se shuru hui yeh pahal aaj ek bade samuday mein badal chuki hai.

Aaj hamare manch par saikdon lekhak aur hazaron pathak jude hue hain. Hum har mahine 5+ open mic events aayojit karte hain jahan naye aur anubhavi dono tarah ke kalakar apni pratibha dikha sakte hain.
